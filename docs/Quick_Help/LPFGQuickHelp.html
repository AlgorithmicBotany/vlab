LPFG Quick Help

General

  • Rotate: Left click and drag
  • Zoom: Middle click and drag OR
    • Cmd + Left click and drag
  • Pan: Shift + Left click and drag
  • Roll: Shift + Middle click and drag

Interaction with models

  • Insert MouseIns() and locate mouse:
    • Shift+Cmd + Left click OR
    • Shift+Alt+Cmd + Left click OR
    • Key 1 + Left click
  • Insert MouseInsPos(MouseStatus) and locate mouse:
    • Alt+Cmd + Left click OR
    • Key 2 + Left click
  • Locate mouse only:
    • Shift+Alt + Left click OR
    • Key 3 + Left click
  • Invoke user-defined menu:
    • Key 4 + Left click
