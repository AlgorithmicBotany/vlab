Cuspy Quick Help

  • Move point: Left click and drag
  • Add/remove point: Cmd + Left click
  • Change point multiplicity: Shift + Left double-click

  • Zoom: Middle click and drag OR
    • Alt + Left click and drag OR
    • Scroll wheel
  • Pan: Shift + Left click and drag

  • Translate background image (if present):
    • Small steps: Arrow keys
    • Large steps: Shift + Arrow keys
  • Translate background image and all points:
    • Small steps: Cmd + Arrow keys
    • Large steps: Shift+Cmd + Arrow keys
