A leaf modeled as a generalized cylinder

Uses numbers to refer to specific functions in the 
functions.fset file. 

xyz.func is an extra function for testing the gallery.

Based on P. Prusinkiewicz, L. Muendermann, R. Karwowski and 
B. Lane, "The use of positional information in the 
modeling of plants", Proceedings of Siggraph 2001.
