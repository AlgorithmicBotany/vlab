Anklets of Krishna

Construction of this and other kolam patterns were 
described by P. Prusinkiewicz and J. Hanan in 
"Lindenmayer systems, fractals, and plants", 
Lecture Notes in Biomathematics 79,
Springer, New York 1989, Chapter 6.2

Note the use of symbols {(4) and }(4) to interpret 
a sequence of turtle locations as a spline curve.
